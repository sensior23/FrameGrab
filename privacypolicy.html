01

Who We Are

FrameGrab ("we", "us", "our") is a browser-based YouTube screenshot and note-taking tool available at framegrab.app. FrameGrab allows users to capture video frames, annotate screenshots, and generate AI-powered notes from YouTube videos entirely within their own browser.

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use FrameGrab.

02

What Data We Collect

FrameGrab is designed with a privacy-first approach. Here is a complete breakdown of what is and is not collected:

✓ We do NOT collect:
Your name, email address, or any account information · Screenshots or video frames you capture · AI notes generated in your session · YouTube URLs you paste · Your IP address (by FrameGrab directly) · Any personally identifiable information
ℹ What is processed locally in your browser:
Screenshots captured via screen-share API · AI-generated notes (sent to Anthropic's API only if you provide your own API key) · Session data saved to your browser's localStorage · Your API key (stored in localStorage on your device only)

We may collect anonymised, aggregated analytics (such as page views and general geographic region) through third-party tools like Google Analytics or AdSense. This data cannot be used to identify you personally.

03

How We Use Your Data

Any data processed by FrameGrab is used exclusively to:

  • Render the application and provide its core features (screenshot capture, AI notes, gallery)
  • Save your session locally so you can resume where you left off (via localStorage)
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense to support the free service
  • Analyse aggregate site usage to improve the product (via anonymised analytics, if applicable)

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal data with any third party for marketing purposes.

04

Local Storage & Session Data

FrameGrab uses your browser's localStorage to save:

  • Your most recent session (video ID and captured screenshots) — so you can restore it on next visit
  • Your dark/light mode preference
  • Whether you have dismissed the disclaimer banner
  • Your Anthropic API key (if you choose to enter one) — stored only on your device, never transmitted to FrameGrab's servers

This data never leaves your browser and is not accessible to us. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser's site data or using the ↺ Reset button in the app.

Note on API Keys: If you provide an Anthropic API key to enable AI notes, that key is stored in your browser's localStorage and is sent directly from your browser to api.anthropic.com only. FrameGrab's servers never receive or store your API key.
05

Third-Party Services

FrameGrab integrates with the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • YouTube (Google LLC) — Videos are embedded via the YouTube IFrame Player API. Your interaction with the embedded player is subject to Google's Privacy Policy and YouTube's Terms of Service.
  • Anthropic — If you choose to enter an Anthropic API key, AI note requests are sent directly from your browser to Anthropic's API endpoint. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdSense — We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. See Section 6 for details.
  • Google Fonts — Fonts are loaded from Google's CDN. Google may collect limited data as part of this request. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Cloudflare CDN — Some JavaScript libraries (e.g. JSZip) are loaded from Cloudflare's CDN. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.
06

Google AdSense & Cookies

FrameGrab uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to serve personalised ads based on your browsing history and interests.

Specifically, Google may:

  • Use cookies to track your visits to FrameGrab and other websites
  • Use the DoubleClick cookie to serve ads based on your prior visits to websites
  • Collect data such as your IP address, browser type, and pages visited

You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings or aboutads.info.

For more information, see Google's Advertising Policies.

Cookie Notice: By using FrameGrab, you consent to the use of cookies by Google AdSense for advertising purposes as described above. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect ad personalisation.
07

YouTube API Services

FrameGrab uses the YouTube IFrame Player API to embed and interact with YouTube videos. By using FrameGrab, you are also bound by:

  • YouTube Terms of Service
  • Google Privacy Policy

FrameGrab does not access, store, or transmit any video content from YouTube. All video data remains within the embedded YouTube player controlled by Google.

Important: Capturing screenshots of YouTube videos may be subject to copyright restrictions. You are solely responsible for ensuring your use of captured content complies with applicable law and YouTube's Terms of Service.

08

Children's Privacy

FrameGrab is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the European Economic Area). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used FrameGrab in a way that has provided personal data, please contact us at the address below and we will take appropriate steps.

09

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Right of access — you can request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Right to erasure — you can request deletion of your data
  • Right to object — you can object to processing of your data for advertising purposes
  • Right to data portability — you can request your data in a portable format

Since FrameGrab stores virtually all data locally in your browser, you can exercise most of these rights yourself by clearing your browser's site data. For any requests regarding third-party data (e.g. Google AdSense), please contact those services directly.

For any other requests, contact us using the details in Section 12.

10

Data Security

Because FrameGrab processes data entirely within your browser:

  • No screenshots or notes are transmitted to or stored on FrameGrab's servers
  • Your API key is stored only in your browser's localStorage and transmitted only to Anthropic directly over HTTPS
  • Session data is stored only on your device

We recommend keeping your browser and operating system up to date, and avoiding shared or public computers when using features that involve sensitive API keys.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of FrameGrab after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
